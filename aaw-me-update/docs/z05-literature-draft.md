## Working Positioning

Product naming update: this launch finish is named **Z05 AuAg**, the AuAg Launch Edition of the Z05 built around the gold/silver foil decoration scheme. Au = gold; Ag = silver.

The AAW Z05 AuAg carries forward the Z-series hybrid in-ear monitor philosophy introduced with the Z06, while reshaping the platform around a new PET diaphragm 10mm dynamic woofer, a user-adjustable sound signature switch system, and forged carbon faceplates with side-specific foil lacing across both faceplate and body shell.

Where the Z06 emphasized AAW's BassFlow Vent Pressure System and dual isobaric miniature woofer architecture, the Z05 should be positioned as the more expressive and user-tunable evolution: powerful dynamic-driver bass, adaptable tuning, daily universal-fit comfort, and a visually distinctive artisanal finish.

## Primary Feature Pillars

### 1. New PET Diaphragm 10mm Dynamic Woofer

The Z05 replaces the Z06's miniature dual-isobaric woofer concept with a newly developed 10mm dynamic woofer using a PET diaphragm. This larger dynamic driver is intended to give Z05 a more physical, elastic, and full-bodied bass response, with stronger air movement and a more natural sense of low-frequency decay.

PET diaphragm material offers a useful balance of rigidity, lightness, and damping behavior. In the Z05, this helps the woofer move with authority while retaining control over transient edges, bass texture, and midrange bleed. The result should be described as bass that is weighty and expressive without overwhelming the rest of the frequency range.

Draft messaging:
- Larger 10mm dynamic woofer for a more physical bass foundation.
- PET diaphragm for balanced stiffness, damping, and responsiveness.
- Designed for impact, texture, and natural decay.
- Tuned to integrate with the hybrid system rather than dominate it.

Technical basis:

The Z05 switch is fundamentally different from the simple switches commonly found on many in-ear monitors. A conventional IEM switch usually opens or closes a single component path, creating a narrow adjustment around one part of the response. The Z05 switch instead changes between two complete crossover circuit states. In practical terms, the switch changes the resistor loading applied to both the low-frequency and high-frequency sections, so the shift is not limited to bass quantity alone. The entire frequency response is rebalanced.

Switch circuit states:

- Blue position: low-frequency resistor path at 20 ohms, high-frequency resistor path at 20 ohms.
- Red position: low-frequency resistor path at 40 ohms, high-frequency resistor path at 0 ohms.

Draft consumer-facing explanation:

The Z05 AuAg introduces AAW's TwinCore Crossover Switch System, a two-position tuning architecture that switches between two complete crossover voicings. Instead of acting as a simple bass boost or treble cut, the system changes how the low-frequency and high-frequency sections are electrically loaded, reshaping the monitor's balance across the full spectrum.

With the blue setting, the Z05 presents a fuller, more grounded tuning with a stronger low-frequency foundation and a smooth, cohesive upper range. With the red setting, the crossover path shifts to a more open and energized presentation, bringing greater upper-frequency presence and a more forward sense of clarity. The result is not one monitor with a minor EQ switch, but one monitor with two deliberately engineered sound signatures.

Short technical copy:

TwinCore is a complete crossover-state switch. Blue mode applies 20 ohms to both low and high frequency paths. Red mode changes the network to 40 ohms on the low-frequency path and 0 ohms on the high-frequency path. Because both ends of the crossover network are reloaded, the response changes across the full frequency range rather than only at one isolated band.

## Draft Full Literature Copy

### Opening

The AAW Z05 AuAg represents the next expression of AAW's Z-series universal in-ear monitor platform. Derived from the design philosophy of the Z06, the Z05 AuAg introduces a new PET diaphragm 10mm dynamic woofer, an adjustable sound signature switch system, a modular swappable-termination cable, forged carbon faceplates, and side-specific foil-laced shell finishing.

Designed for listeners who want both emotional musicality and technical control, the Z05 AuAg balances powerful dynamic-driver bass with the clarity, spaciousness, and precision expected from AAW's hybrid in-ear monitor architecture. Its switchable tuning system allows the sound to adapt to different listening preferences, while its universal shell is shaped for comfort, seal, and long-session wearability.

### PET Diaphragm 10mm Dynamic Woofer

At the heart of the Z05 is a newly developed 10mm dynamic woofer using a PET diaphragm. Compared with the compact isobaric bass system of the Z06, the Z05's larger woofer is designed to move more air and deliver a stronger sense of physical low-frequency presence.

The PET diaphragm was selected for its balance of responsiveness, control, and damping. This allows the woofer to produce bass with weight and texture while maintaining clean transient behavior. Low frequencies are intended to feel full and expressive, but still integrated with the mids and highs rather than detached from the rest of the monitor.

The result is a bass presentation that is powerful, rounded, and musical, with enough control to preserve detail in complex recordings.

### TwinCore Crossover Switch System

The Z05 AuAg introduces AAW's TwinCore Crossover Switch System, a two-position tuning architecture that changes between two complete crossover circuit states. This is not a conventional single-component switch. Instead of simply opening or closing one path, the Z05 switch changes the resistor loading applied to both the low-frequency and high-frequency sections of the crossover.

In the blue position, the low-frequency and high-frequency paths are each loaded at 20 ohms. In the red position, the low-frequency path changes to 40 ohms while the high-frequency path is shorted to 0 ohms. Because the switch changes both sections of the network, the resulting tuning shift affects the full response curve rather than only one isolated frequency band.

This gives the Z05 two distinct characters. Blue mode offers a fuller, more grounded presentation with a cohesive low-frequency foundation. Red mode opens the upper response for a clearer, more energetic presentation. The listener can choose the mode that best matches the music, source, mood, or preferred tonal balance.

### 3D Printed Enclosure and Acoustic Precision

Like the Z06, the Z05 benefits from AAW's digitized acoustic design process. Driver placement, chamber geometry, nozzle path, and shell ergonomics can be refined through simulation and precision manufacturing before reaching final production.

This approach allows the Z05 enclosure to serve as an acoustic component, not simply as a housing. The internal chamber supports the behavior of the 10mm woofer, while the nozzle and driver arrangement help maintain phase coherence, response control, and consistent unit-to-unit performance.

Precision 3D manufacturing also supports the Z05's universal-fit comfort. By shaping the shell around AAW's accumulated fitment knowledge, the Z05 aims to provide a secure and comfortable fit for a wide range of listeners.

### Forged Carbon And Foil-Laced Shell Craft

The Z05 AuAg's appearance is intentionally more dramatic and artisanal than a conventional universal monitor. Forged carbon faceplates form the visual foundation, while gold and silver foil details are laced through the corresponding shells: gold on the right ear, silver on the left. Instead of limiting the finish to the faceplate, the foil treatment continues through the whole body shell on each side, creating a layered visual texture with depth and movement.

The result is an in-ear monitor that looks refined, technical, and handcrafted at once. The foil-laced treatment gives each pair a distinct character, while the engraved AAW and Z05 markings keep the design grounded in the product identity.

### Modular Cable System

The Z05 AuAg includes a 2-pin detachable cable with swappable termination options for multiple listening environments. Users can connect through 3.5mm stereo for broad compatibility, 4.4mm balanced for dedicated audio players and DAC/amps, or Type-C for modern mobile and computer setups.

This modular approach reduces the need to carry multiple cables and makes the Z05 easier to integrate into different source chains. Whether the listener is using a smartphone, laptop, portable DAC, or balanced digital audio player, the Z05 AuAg cable system is designed to remain flexible.
