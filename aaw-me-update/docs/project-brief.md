# Project Brief

## Purpose

Update www.aaw.me so customers can understand AAW's product lines, choose the right in-ear monitor path, and move confidently toward purchase or inquiry.

## Brand Slogan

Those who listen will find the way.

善听者，终能找到道路。

## Audiences

- Musicians who need reliable stage monitoring, fit security, serviceability, and clear custom-fit guidance.
- Audiophiles who care about sound signature, driver architecture, product hierarchy, build quality, and reviews.
- Existing IEM owners looking for reshell, repair, or accessory support.
- Dealers and international buyers who need location, availability, and contact clarity.

## Desired Outcomes

- Clearer homepage hierarchy and stronger first-screen positioning.
- Simpler category paths for For Musician, For Audiophile, TOTL, Wireless, and Accessories.
- Better product comparison support.
- Stronger custom IEM education and custom-builder handoff.
- Cleaner service pages for reshell and support.
- Improved SEO foundations for product, category, and service pages.

## Scope Candidates

- Homepage redesign or content refresh.
- Navigation and menu restructure.
- Product page content and template improvements.
- Collection page filtering, sorting, and merchandising review.
- Custom builder flow review.
- Where to Buy and Contact Us experience updates.
- Gallery curation and presentation.
- SEO metadata, structured content, and analytics QA.

## Out of Scope Until Confirmed

- Full replatforming away from Shopify.
- New brand identity.
- New photography or rendered product visuals.
- Checkout customization beyond Shopify-supported changes.
- Third-party app replacement.

## Success Measures

- A first-time visitor can identify the right product or service path within one minute.
- Product pages answer fit, sound, compatibility, configuration, warranty, and purchase questions without requiring support contact.
- Custom-fit and universal-fit paths are distinct but connected.
- Dealer and contact paths are easy to find from mobile and desktop.
- Phase-one changes can be shipped without blocking on a full redesign.
