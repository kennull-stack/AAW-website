# Open Questions

## Business

- What is the main goal of the update: more direct purchases, more custom inquiries, better dealer support, brand refresh, or all of these?
- Which products should be prioritized on the homepage?
- Are there upcoming product launches that should shape the structure?
- Which markets and currencies matter most?

## Platform

- Should this project include Shopify theme code, or only planning/content until theme access is available?
- What Shopify theme is currently running?
- Are there custom apps or integrations behind Custom Builder, dealer pages, reviews, email, or analytics?
- What is the deployment process and who approves publishing?

## Content

- Do we have current product photography and technical specs?
- Are there official descriptions for each sound signature?
- Do we have reviews, artist/customer proof, lab/manufacturing imagery, or measurement data we can use?
- Should the tone lean more technical/audiophile, musician-practical, luxury craft, or a blend by page type?

## Customer Experience

- What should happen when a visitor selects Custom Fit?
- Should users compare models by sound signature, driver config, price, use case, or fit type?
- Are reshell and repair services available globally?
- Should Where to Buy emphasize authorized dealers, regions, or direct online purchase?
