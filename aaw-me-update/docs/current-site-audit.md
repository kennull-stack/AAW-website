# Current Site Audit

Audit started: May 22, 2026.

## Observed Pages And Flows

Source checked: [www.aaw.me](https://www.aaw.me)

### Homepage

- Navigation includes Home, Custom Builder, Get Started, Where to Buy, Gallery, and Contact Us.
- Homepage hero/content areas currently feature:
  - Z06 Universal In-Ear Monitor.
  - Canary Pro Hybrid In-Ear Monitor.
  - AAW Laboratory Tour.
  - Universal Fit.
  - Custom Fit.
  - Reshell.
  - Accessories.
- Category links visible: For Musician, For Audiophile, TOTL, Wireless, Accessories.

### Early Observations

- The site has valuable entry points, but the relationship between product categories, fit types, and services should be made more explicit.
- The homepage appears to mix product promotion, brand story, fit/service education, and category navigation. This can work, but the hierarchy needs to be very intentional.
- The international currency and language UI appears prominent in extracted page text; mobile and desktop visual behavior should be checked directly before making design decisions.
- The custom-builder path is likely a major conversion flow and should be audited separately.
