Warranty& Refit

Every AAW monitor is built and finished by hand in Singapore. We stand behind that work with a 1-year limited warranty, a 30-day refit period, and flat-rate repair options for products out of coverage.

1-Year WarrantyCovers defects in materials and workmanship from the date of purchase. Transferable between owners.
30-Day RefitIf your custom monitor does not fit properly, we will adjust or rebuild it within 30 days of delivery.
Flat Rate Repair SGD150Out-of-warranty repairs at a fixed price for most active and discontinued AAW models.
Singapore WorkshopAll repairs, reshells, and warranty work are performed at 76 Playfair Rd by the same team that built your monitors.

Coverage

What We Cover

Our limited warranty protects against defects in materials and workmanship. It is straightforward, transferable, and begins the moment you receive your monitors.

Refit Period

AAW provides a 30-day refit period against poor fit from the delivery date. If your custom in-ear monitor does not seal or sit comfortably, contact us and we will make it right.

Limited Warranty

This limited warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship in the products. It is valid for 1 year upon purchase by the customer and is fully transferable between owners.

Extended Warranty

Customers can acquire an extra 3 months of warranty coverage once the product is successfully registered on the AAW website. Register promptly after purchase to extend your protection.

What is not covered by this limited warranty

External causes such as accident, abuse, misuse, or problems with electrical power
Unauthorized servicing or modification by a third party
Usage that is not in accordance with product specifications
Failure to follow product instructions or failure to perform preventive maintenance
Products with missing or altered service tags or serial numbers
Products for which we have not received payment
Normal degradation in aesthetics over time
Demo units purchased from a distributor or dealer
Return shipping costs to us and back to the customer

How to Claim

Warranty Claims

Filing a warranty claim is simple. Email us to request an RMA number, pack your product safely, and ship it to our Singapore workshop. We will inspect and respond promptly.

Request an RMA

Email support@aaw.me to request an RMA number. We will respond with return instructions within one business day. Do not ship your product without an RMA number.

1

Pack with care

Return the product to us in safe and well-protected packaging to avoid further damage in transit. Use the original case or equivalent protection.

2

Include documentation

Include your warranty card, proof of purchase, a note describing the defect, and only the defective item. Exclude non-defective accessories such as cables, ear tips, and the carrying case.

3

Return both sides

Always return both sides of the IEM, even if only one side appears defective. This allows us to verify channel matching and ensure both monitors perform identically after repair.

Register for +3 Months

Once your product is successfully registered on the AAW website, an additional 3 months of warranty coverage is added automatically. Registration takes less than two minutes and requires your order number and serial number.

Out of Warranty

Flat Rate Repair

If your AAW monitor is out of warranty, our flat-rate repair programme offers a fixed-price path back to working condition. Most active and discontinued models qualify.

Eligibility

All AAW branded UIEM and CIEM active models or discontinued models are eligible, with the exception of Nebula 1, Nebula 2, Q, and products purchased more than 3 years ago. Products older than 3 years will be evaluated and quoted on a case-by-case basis.

Flat Rate SGD150

What can be repaired

  • Cracked or shattered enclosure
  • Damaged socket
  • Damaged driver
  • Blocked nozzle
  • Cable defects (refer to Null Audio section)

What cannot be repaired

  • Missing one side or both sides of IEM
  • Product severely damaged with missing components such as drivers
  • Product intentionally abused
  • Water-related damage
  • Normal cosmetic degradation
  • Damaged packaging, ear tips, carry case, and other accessories
Beyond Repair

If AAW determines the IEM is beyond repair — for reasons including but not limited to unavailable repair parts or products designed with low reparability — it will be at AAW's discretion to reject the repair. For products purchased more than 3 years ago, AAW will evaluate and advise repair cost on a case-by-case basis.

Additional Services

Ownership Transfer & Trade-in

Whether you are passing your monitors to a new owner or upgrading to a newer model, we offer two structured programmes to keep your AAW investment moving forward.

Flat Rate SGD200

Ownership Transfer

Reshell an existing AAW UIEM or CIEM into a new CIEM with the new owner's ear impressions. Reshelling with new ear impressions for the existing owner also qualifies under this service.

Eligibility

All AAW branded UIEM and CIEM active or discontinued models, except Nebula 1, Nebula 2, Q, and products purchased more than 3 years ago.

Additional Options

Faceplate inserts, artwork, engravings, and other custom options are charged at the same rate as new CIEM orders.

Defective Units

If the IEM is defective before submission for ownership transfer, an aggregated repair charge of SGD150 will be added.

Warranty

3-month extended warranty on the shell and socket only. Driver issues are not covered under this service warranty.

Upgrade Programme

Trade-in Service

Trade in your existing AAW UIEM or CIEM toward a new CIEM or UIEM. The trade-in value is calculated against the new model's retail price.

Eligibility

All AAW branded UIEM and CIEM active or discontinued models are eligible. There are no model exclusions for trade-in.

Cost Formula

Total Amount = New Model MSRP − (Trade-in Unit MSRP × 30%). The new model MSRP must be greater than the trade-in unit MSRP. No downgrades are permitted.

Additional Options

Faceplate inserts, artwork, engravings, and other custom options are charged at the same rate as new CIEM orders.

Defective Units & Warranty

Defective products can be submitted for trade-in and will be treated with no difference. The new CIEM or UIEM will enjoy the full standard warranty.
