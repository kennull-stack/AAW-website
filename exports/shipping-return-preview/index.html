Policy

Returns

14-Day Window

Our policy lasts 14 days. If 14 days have gone by since confirmation of delivery (based on tracking information), unfortunately we cannot offer a refund or exchange.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. A receipt or proof of purchase is required.

Non-Returnable Items

Gift cards and Custom In-Ear Monitors cannot be returned. Please do not send your purchase back to the manufacturer.

Partial refunds may be granted for items not in original condition, damaged, or missing parts for reasons not due to our error, or items returned more than 14 days after delivery.

Process

Refunds & Exchanges

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will email you to confirm receipt and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed and credited to your original method of payment within a certain number of days.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received a refund, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company — it may take time before the refund is officially posted. Next, contact your bank. If you still haven't received it, reach out to us at support@aaw.me.

Sale Items & Gifts

Only regular priced items may be refunded; sale items cannot be refunded. If the item was marked as a gift, you'll receive a gift credit for the value of your return. Otherwise, the refund goes to the original purchaser.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it for the same item, email us at support@aaw.me and send your item to the address below.

Return Shipping Cost

You are responsible for paying your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted. We recommend using our prepaid FedEx return shipping label for safety and the lowest cost.

Shipping Insurance

If you are shipping an item over $75, consider using a traceable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance. We do not guarantee that we will receive your returned item without tracking.
